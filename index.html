Speakers

Huda Idrees

Huda is the Founder and CEO of Dot Health, a real-time personal health data platform. With a background in engineering from the University of Toronto and a prolific career at some of Toronto's best known startups, she hopes to transform the way people access and control their own health information for good. A strong advocate for women and minorities in technology, Huda's work been profiled in numerous media outlets including The Globe and Mail, Metro News, CNBC, and Metro Morning. Huda's work in the field of entrepreneurship recently earned her a spot as a Top 5 CEO in Toronto Life Magazine. She was also recognized as one of Top 25 Women of Influence in 2017. Huda serves on the Business Innovation and Development committee at Sinai Health System, and the Canadian Federal Economic Strategy Table for Health and Biosciences.
